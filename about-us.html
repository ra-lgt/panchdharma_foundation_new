Supporting Our Cause Together

Support Our Mission and Make a Difference

The PANCHDHARMA Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation headquartered in LUCKNOW India. The Foundation strives to eliminate classroom hunger by implementing the PM POSHAN (Mid-Day Meal) Programme. It provides nutritious meals to children studying in government schools and government-aided schools. PANCHDHARMA also aims to counter malnutrition and support the Right to Education of children hailing from socio-economically challenging backgrounds.
